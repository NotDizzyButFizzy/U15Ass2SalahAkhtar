Events & Projects

Celebrate, Connect & Experience Slovak Culture

At Slovenské Vzdelávacie Centrum – South Manchester, we believe Slovak culture is something to experience—not just learn about. That’s why we organise a variety of events for families, children, adults, and anyone interested in connecting with Slovak traditions, community, and language.

Whether you’re a parent wanting your child to engage in cultural activities, a Slovak abroad looking to stay connected, or someone simply curious about Slovak heritage—you’re always welcome here.
