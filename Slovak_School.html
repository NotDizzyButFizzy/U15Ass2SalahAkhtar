Slovak School

A vibrant, inclusive, and engaging place to learn the Slovak language and culture.

Our Approach

We are a team of qualified educators with extensive experience in both Slovak and UK educational settings. Our goal is to create a student-centred, culturally enriching learning environment where language learning naturally integrates with culture and real-life application.

Integrated Language & Cultural Learning

We believe language and culture go hand-in-hand. Our curriculum aligns with cultural events and workshops, offering students a chance to experience Slovak traditions beyond the classroom. These workshops are also available separately for members interested in culture only.
