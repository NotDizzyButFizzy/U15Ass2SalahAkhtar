Welcome to Our Journal

Welcome to the Journal of Slovenské Vzdelávacie Centrum – South Manchester. Here, we share valuable educational materials, updates from our school and community, and inspiring stories that reflect our mission to preserve and celebrate Slovak language and culture abroad.

Work Book – „Pracovný zošit“

A seasonal learning resource designed by our educators to support learners of all ages in their Slovak language journey.

📘 What's Inside:

  • Grammar & Vocabulary Exercises – Age-appropriate, structured activities that align with our curriculum
  • Cultural Activities – Tasks exploring Slovak holidays, traditions, and history
  • Creative Writing & Reading – Fun exercises that apply Slovak in real-life situations
  • Games & Fun Sheets – Keeping learning playful and interactive

How to Access:

  • 🖥️ Free Online Version – Available to all our students and members
  • 🖨️ Downloadable PDF – A printable version is available for purchase (link coming soon)

This workbook is perfect for classroom practice, home learning, or preparation for ‘komisionálne skúšky’.
